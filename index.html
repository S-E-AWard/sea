Our 5-Point Plan

What We Stand For

Roads and Infrastructure

Lobby for murram and tarmacking of key feeder roads connecting SEA villages to markets and schools.

Clean Water Access

Push for borehole drilling and piped-water extensions so every home has reliable, clean water year round.

Education Bursaries

Transparent bursary allocation to keep every child in school, covering both secondary and tertiary level support.

Farmers Support

Facilitate subsidised inputs, storage facilities, and market linkages for SEA Ward farmers.

Health and Safety

Ensure dispensaries are stocked, staffed, and that women and youth health programmes are fully funded.

Accountable Leadership

Monthly public barazas, published ward budgets, and direct community oversight of every project.
